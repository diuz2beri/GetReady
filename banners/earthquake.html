EARTHQUAKE?


What is a Earthquake?

An earthquake is caused by the breaking and shifting of rock beneath the earth’s surface. Ground shaking from earthquakes can collapse buildings and bridges, disrupt gas, electric and phone services and, sometimes trigger landslides, avalanches, flash floods, fires and huge destructive ocean waves (tsunamis).
